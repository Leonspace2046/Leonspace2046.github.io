How to complete Geralt of Rivia Quests

Geralt of Rivia has arrived, and with tough quests and a limited time to complete them, players are struggling to catch on. Here's how to get ahead of the rest and complete them early.

The first quest is activate 5 different augments in different matches. You cannot do 5 augments in the same match. To do it quickly, jump off the Battle Bus as soon as possible and wait for an augment to be available. Apply any augment, leave the match and repeat until you have done it 5 times in 5 different matches. When you do this, you will be able to claim a Geralt of Rivia Loading Screen.
