Breeze coming back to competitive in Valorant after map changes

Breeze is one of the most disliked maps in Valorant because of its big bomb sites and large rotations when playing on Attack. You can dominate most major areas and Defence is forced to scurry around the edges of the site, getting picked easily.


Due to this issue, Riot Games has been forced to remove it from Competitive to fix these issues and try to even out the sides.


Luckily, Riot has addressed this and has made an official announcement saying that Breeze will return at the start of Episode 7, Act 2.
