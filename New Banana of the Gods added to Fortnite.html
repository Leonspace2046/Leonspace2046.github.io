Crawls out of the bush I've been hiding in

I'M BACK BABY

Epic Games has added a new healing item to Fortnite called the Banana of the Gods in Patch 29.10. This item may be slightly overrated, though.


This item is only available in the Legendary tier, and while it does increase your sprint speed and jump height for a brief amount of time, the effects are pretty small and don't really benefit you much.

Another thing is that, unlike the Slurp Juice, it does not heal your shields as well. It just keeps healing your HP bar, which could be useful if you consumed many and combined them with medkits, floppers, and chug splashes in a heal-off. Otherwise, you're better off taking the medkits, to be honest.
