It's probably a good idea to experiment with all the maps to get to know the tasks, best hiding places and vents. In Among Us, when the impostor/s kill a crewmate, a crewmate might report the dead body or the impostor could self-report. When a dead body is reported, the game pauses and everyone can try to find out who the impostor is by spreading information in the chat box. When you've come to a decision or when time is up, you will have to vote. If you are unsure of who to vote, you can also press the skip. If you find out who the impostor is or you have crucial information, you can go to the cafeteria and do an emergency meeting, which is basically like dead body reported. The crewmates' objective is to finish their tasks and/or vote out the impostor. The impostors' objective is to kill all the crewmates.

ROBLOX

I haven't included a video for Roblox yet because what many people don't realise is that Roblox is like a massive amusement park. It has MILLIONS of games, all in one game. If you're new to Roblox, why don't you come up with an epic avatar? On the Roblox home page, look at the different icons. There's home, discover, a picture of your current avatar and a dot dot dot icon. Press on the picture of your avatar. Now select either customise or shop. If you want to just change your avatar, go to customise, and if you want to buy different accessories, jumpers, outfits and far, far more, go to shop. Most of the items in the shop cost Robux, Roblox's in-game currency. You can buy Robux on the online Robux shop (will explain later) or you can top up with a Roblox gift card, available at many toy shops' gaming section (e.g. Smyths Toys Superstore). Once you've come up with a good avatar, it's time to get started!

The Battle Pass

The Battle Pass in Fortnite usually costs 950 vBucks, Fortnite's in-game credit. (To get vBucks, you can buy them from the in-game vBucks section, buy a git card at the gaming section of stores like Smyths, or claim them from the free section of the battle pass, that you still need to level up to claim. More on vBucks later.) However, INSIDE The Battle Pass has an amount of vBucks between 1000 and 1200. You can use vBucks in the in-game shop, which has skins, bundles, emotes, back blings, gliders, and more! However, unlike other games, these products do not improve gameplay. The only way to improve your skill is to practice. If you buy The Battle Pass, you'll be using tons of battle stars, so try to get to a high level before buying it. The higher the level, the faster you can claim further pages! The Battle Pass has a selected amount of every type of product from the in-game Fortnite shop, which makes it a pretty good offer.

vBucks

vBucks can be aquired, as explained earlier, from the in-game vBucks section, buy a gift card at the gaming sections at stores like Smyths, or claim them from the free section of the battle pass. No matter what websites tell you, vBucks CANNOT be aquired for free, so don't give away any personal information.

The Dragon Ball Update

In The Dragon Ball update, There were two new mythics added: The Kamehameha and The Nimbus Cloud.

The Kamehameha is good if you need to shoot an opponent who is hyperactively building and moving. The Nimbus Cloud is good for getting away from a battle you think you'll lose.

Along with the Dragon Ball Update, A Dragon Ball Adventure Island game was added in Discover.

In the Dragon Ball Adventure Island, you can do PvP in a special arena where you will have a Nimbus Cloud, Kamehameha and a gun. There are also bottles scattered in plain sight across the map. Each bottle nets you 38 XP. You can also fly around on a Nimbus Cloud onto a cloud.

Star Wars

Star Wars was added in Fortnite. There were two new weapons: The E-11 Blaster and Darth Vader's Lightsaber, both of which are mythics.

The E-11 Blaster can be obtained by opening Imperial Chests in the Imperial Docking Bays. There are five Imperial Docking Bays in total, and they are located in the yellow circles of the following image:

Imperial Chests not only include the E-11 Blaster, they also have random legendarys and epics. However, if you enter an Imperial Docking Bay, the chances of you getting attacked by Darth Vader and/or Imperial Stormtroopers are higher than any other location on the Island.

Darth Vader's Lightsaber cannot be obtained from Imperial Chests, so the only way to get Darth Vader's Lightsaber is to kill Darth Vader or to kill someone who has already done so. If you're trying to kill Darth Vader, You should probably get an E-11 Blaster or an even better weapon. Darth Vader can throw things at you with The Force, use The Force to bring you to him, Force-choke you and block your bullets. The only place where he can't block bullets is his head, which also happens to be the place for a critical shot. You can still shoot at his body and he still will take some damage. Darth Vader is also flanked by two Imperial Stormtroopers.
