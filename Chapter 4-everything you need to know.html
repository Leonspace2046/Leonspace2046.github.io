After the Chapter 4 live event, MrBeast was added to Fortnite. Remember this in the live event?



Anyway, Here are the new weapons:

Thunder ShotgunA brand-new shotgun
Red-Eye Assault Rifle Similar to the MK-7 Assault rifle, but a lower rate of fire
Ex-Caliber Rifle Shoots a sword at you and then explodes
Maven Auto Shotgun A bit weaker than the Thunder Shotgun
Twin Mag SMG Two magazines are better than one!
Tactical Pistol A pistol that fires so fast that most players are pleased to see a weapon that is a pistol.

There are a bunch of Unvaulted Weapons, too, the Assault Rifle, the Rocket Laucher and a bunch of bows.

In Chapter 3, we had Slurp Barrels. They would give you slurp juice. Now we have Slap Barrels. They give you Slurp Juice, which also regenerates 20 health or shields, but they also give you infinite sprint for a while. Slap trucks, however, give a much greater amount of health or shields, and still give you the infinite sprint for a while reward.
