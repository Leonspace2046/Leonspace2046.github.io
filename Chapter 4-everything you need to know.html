Fortnite Chapter 4 is out now. With a couple of new weapons such as the Thunder Shotgun, it looks like we're in for a fun Chapter.

After the Chapter 4 live event, people are talking about a possibility that MrBeast might come to Fortnite. Near the end of the live event, when The Paradigm was looking out at everything that the Zero Point had pulled in, you could see MrBeast being pulled in, and the camera even closed up to him and pulled away really quickly. He was also floating above a biome with lots of trees, so that could possibly be a reference to when he planted 1 million trees.



Anyway, Here are the new weapons:

Thunder ShotgunA brand-new shotgun
Red-Eye Assault Rifle Similar to the MK-7 Assault rifle, but a lower rate of fire
Ex-Caliber Rifle Shoots a sword at you and then explodes
Maven Auto Shotgun A bit weaker than the Thunder Shotgun
Twin Mag SMG Two magazines are better than one!
Tactical Pistol A pistol that fires so fast that most players are pleased to see a weapon that is a pistol.

There are a buch of Unvaulted Weapons, too, such as the Assault Rifle.
