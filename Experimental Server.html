Fortnite downtime today (February 14): When will servers be back up?

By Matthew Wilkins
Modified Feb 14, 2023 08:57 GMT
Follow Us

The Fortnite update v23.40 will go live today (February 14, 2023). As is custom, game servers will be taken offline by 3:30 am Eastern Time to allow the developers to implement changes in-game. Thirty minutes after this happens, the update will begin at 4:00 am Eastern Time.

For those who play into the wee hours of the morning, it would be wise to log off before the servers are taken offline. While those in BR mode can stay until the very end without any repercussions, Save The World players need to be a bit more careful. If the session ends without completing the mission, progress will be lost.

Since a brand new system/mechanic is being added to the game, the downtime for the Fortnite update v23.40 may take longer than usual. The developers will have to do one final test run before switching the servers on again.

That said, aside from the new system/mechanic, there's not much information as to what to expect during the update. With Chapter 4 Season 1 coming to an end, it's more of a routine maintenance than an actual full-fledged update.

Fixes and content changes for Fortnite v23.40 patch

While the "Most Wanted" posters that popped up around the island were supposedly hinting at the theme of Chapter 4 Season 2, things have turned out differently.

It seems that this will now be a brand new system/mechanic being added to the game that's very similar to the "Reduce Heat" feature in GTA 5. However, it's still not clear how things will function in-game

That said, based on the words used by the developers in their tweet regarding the update, players will be able to "break open the vault." At the moment, this could mean one of two things:

  1. The new system/mechanic will re-introduce vaults in some manner.
  2. The new system/mechanic will allow players to remove a mark placed on them while undertaking a special challenge/quest related to vaults.

On that note, it's not known which "vault" is being referred to here. With Chapter 4 Season 1 being medieval-themed, there are no high-tech vaults on the island. In fact, there are no banks as well. It will be interesting to see what the developers have planned.

While the former two are self explanatory, the latter is unique in design and will function as a potent build-destroying weapon.

New cosmetics and weekly challenges will also be added to the game. Regarding cosmetics, a new Level-Up Pack may potentially get added in as well. Lastly, based on the information obtained from the official Trello Board, numerous bug fixes will be implemented in the game during the update v23.40.
