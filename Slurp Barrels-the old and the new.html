New and old Slurp Barrels-the facts

Slurp Barrels are a useful way to get health and shields in Fortnite. There is a new type of slurp barrel in Chapter 4, called Slurp Juice.

Slurp Juice can be found around the Island, along with Slurp Trucks.


Slurp Barrels and Slurp Juice regenerate 20 health or shields.
