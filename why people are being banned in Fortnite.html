Why people are being banned in Fortnite

    With the release of Chapter 4, Fortnite players have been exposing all kinds of glitches. One of the most sought-after, though, are XP glitches. XP glitches will allow players to get XP, often more than you could usually get. Surprisingly, Epic hasn't been doing much about XP glitches, but then a few days ago some players recieved a ban from Fortnite for about 1 day.

    Players were banned because of a Discord bot they used, which allowed them to play any Limited Time Modes, and as many times as they wanted. It also allowed players to gather infinite Slap Berries, which give XP every time you pick them up. However, Epic found out about this glitch and banned the players who used the Discord Bot for about 1 day.


    According to users of this Discord Bot, it wasn't even worth it. The users of the Discord Bot got banned for about 1 day and only got about 1 level.
